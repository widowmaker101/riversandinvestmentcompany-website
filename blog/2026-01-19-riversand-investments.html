Riversand Expands Portfolio with Strategic Investments in Innovative Nigerian Startups

January 19, 2026 • 8 min read • Investments

In the dynamic landscape of Nigeria's startup ecosystem, Riversand Investment Company Limited is proud to announce its latest round of strategic investments. As a leading venture capital and private equity firm based in Abuja, we are committed to fueling the growth of early-stage, growth-focused, and unique companies that are reshaping industries and solving real-world problems. Today, we spotlight three groundbreaking platforms that align perfectly with our investment thesis: FlatShareNaija.com, Henleys Analytica, and RelianceHR. These investments not only underscore our confidence in Nigeria's tech innovation but also highlight our role in empowering entrepreneurs to scale and succeed.

FlatShareNaija.com: Revolutionizing Urban Living Through Smart Flatsharing

Urbanization in Nigeria is accelerating at an unprecedented pace. With over 50% of the population now living in cities like Lagos, Abuja, and Port Harcourt, affordable housing has become a pressing challenge. Enter FlatShareNaija.com, a pioneering flatsharing platform that connects people with spare rooms to those seeking affordable lease options. This is not just a marketplace; it's a community-driven solution that promotes efficient use of space, reduces housing costs, and fosters social connections in bustling urban environments.

Founded by visionary entrepreneurs who recognized the gap in Nigeria's rental market, FlatShareNaija.com enables homeowners and tenants to list and find spare rooms with ease. The platform's user-friendly interface, advanced matching algorithms, and built-in verification systems ensure safe and reliable transactions. In 2025 alone, the Nigerian real estate market grew by 15% (according to PwC reports), driven by digital platforms like this. By investing in FlatShareNaija.com, Riversand is backing a company that is democratizing housing access, potentially reducing urban poverty, and creating new revenue streams for thousands of Nigerians.

Our partnership goes beyond capital. Riversand is providing strategic guidance on scaling operations, expanding to new cities, and integrating AI for better user matching. We believe FlatShareNaija.com has the potential to become the go-to platform for urban dwellers, similar to how Airbnb transformed global lodging. As Nigeria's population is projected to reach 400 million by 2050, solutions like this are not just innovative—they're essential.

Henleys Analytica: Unlocking Data Insights with AI Power

In an era where data is the new oil, businesses need tools to extract value from vast amounts of information. Henleys Analytica is an AI-powered analytics platform that empowers users to gain deep insights into their data, driving informed decision-making across industries. From small businesses analyzing customer trends to large corporations optimizing supply chains, Henleys Analytica democratizes advanced analytics with intuitive interfaces and powerful AI algorithms.

With Nigeria's digital economy expected to contribute 25% to GDP by 2025 (NITDA reports), the demand for data analytics tools is skyrocketing. Henleys Analytica stands out by offering real-time dashboards, predictive modeling, and customizable reports—all powered by machine learning. Our investment in Henleys Analytica reflects Riversand's commitment to tech-driven solutions that enhance business efficiency and competitiveness.

Through this partnership, Riversand is helping Henleys scale its user base, integrate with popular enterprise software, and expand into African markets. The platform has already helped users uncover insights that have led to 30% average cost savings in operations (based on early user feedback). As AI continues to revolutionize industries, Henleys Analytica is positioned to be a leader in Nigeria's data revolution, and we're excited to be part of their journey.

RelianceHR: Transforming HR Management with AI Innovation

Human resources is the backbone of any organization, but traditional HR processes are often time-consuming and error-prone. RelianceHR is an AI-powered human resource management platform that streamlines HR functions, from recruitment and onboarding to performance tracking and payroll. By leveraging AI, RelianceHR automates routine tasks, provides actionable insights, and improves employee engagement—making HR more efficient and strategic.

Nigeria's workforce is one of the youngest and fastest-growing in the world, with over 60% under 30 (World Bank data). However, many businesses struggle with HR scalability. RelianceHR addresses this with features like AI-driven talent matching, automated compliance checks, and predictive analytics for employee retention. In a 2025 survey by Deloitte, 75% of Nigerian companies reported HR digitalization as a top priority, and RelianceHR is at the forefront of this shift.

Riversand's investment in RelianceHR is a testament to our belief in AI's transformative power for business operations. We're supporting their growth with expertise in market expansion and product development, aiming to make RelianceHR the standard for HR management in Africa. Early adopters have reported 40% reductions in HR administrative time, allowing leaders to focus on what matters most: people and growth.
