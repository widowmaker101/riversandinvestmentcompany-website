About Riversand

Founded in 2016, Riversand Investment Company Limited is a leading private investment firm headquartered in Abuja, Nigeria.

We specialize in early-stage venture capital and growth-stage private equity, partnering with exceptional founders building category-defining companies across Africa’s most promising sectors.

Our team combines deep local market knowledge with global investment experience, providing not just capital — but strategic guidance, network access, and operational support to help portfolio companies scale rapidly and sustainably.
